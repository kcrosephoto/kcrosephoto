About The Photographer


Kristen “KC” is a photographer based in the Adirondacks of New York, inspired by the region’s vast landscapes and abundant wildlife. Living among mountains, forests, and waterways, she is motivated by a deep love for the outdoors and a desire to capture quiet moments in nature. Through her photography, KC shares the beauty, serenity, and wild character of the Adirondacks, inviting others to experience the area as she sees it.
